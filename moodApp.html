Mood-tracking App UX Research & Design

A UX research project aimed at developing design recommendations for a mood-tracking app that centres on the user and offers high levels of customisability and flexibility, addressing current market gaps in mood-app tracking personalisation.

Included: Competitor analysis (2 direct and 1 indirect), literature review, survey (20+ responses), user interviews (3x), thematic network mapping, user personas (2x), user journey, design recommendations, and case study presentation slides.
