Mood-tracking App UX Research & Design

A UX research project focused on generating design recommendations for a user-centred mood-tracking app, emphasising customisability and flexibility to address gaps in personalisation found in existing apps. The final case study slides are embedded below, and the specific deliverables can be viewed at the Figma link below.

Included:
  • Competitor analysis (2 direct, 1 indirect)
  • Literature review
  • Survey (20+ responses)
  • User interviews (3x)
  • Thematic network mapping
  • User personas (2x)
  • User journey
  • Design recommendations
  • Case study presentation slides
