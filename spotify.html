Spotify UX Research & Design Project

A comprehensive research and design project aimed at enhancing the social interactions on Spotify. Designed in Figma. I collaborated on this project with a class peer, however, my work primarily focused on the social analytics features. The final case study slides are embeded below, and the specific deliverables can be viewed at the Figma link below.

Included: Competitor analysis (5 direct, 3 indirect), usability audit, survey (60+ responses), user interviews (6x), affinity mapping, user personas (2x), user journeys (2x), thematic network mapping, creative matrix, prioritization matrix, feature roadmaps (2x), storyboards (2x), lofi/highfi wireframing, lofi/highfi prototyping, and case study presentation slides.
